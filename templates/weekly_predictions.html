Understanding the Prediction Model

The weekly food quantity prediction model uses machine learning to analyze patterns in historical data, including:

  • Attendance patterns - How many students typically attend each meal on specific days
  • Consumption rates - How much food is typically consumed per student
  • Day-specific trends - Variations in consumption patterns across different days of the week
  • Meal-specific trends - Differences in consumption between breakfast, lunch, and dinner

The model learns from past patterns to provide accurate predictions for future meal planning. As more data is collected, the predictions will become increasingly accurate.
