Menu Management Information

The weekly menu is used for meal planning and preparation. Each menu item is identified by a unique meal name that combines the day, meal type, and a unique identifier.

For example: Mon_Break_1 for Monday Breakfast.

These meal names are used throughout the system to track attendance, food preparation, and waste analysis.
